Hi, my name is Juan Octavio Serrano Navarrete (JOSN)

I DEFINE MY SELF AS AN ENGINEER & PROJECT MANAGER FOCUSED ON INDUSTRTY 4.0 OR DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

I4.0 & DT are terms (concepts) used to describe the integration of advanced technologies, such as the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), big data analytics, robotics, Additive Manufacturing and other industrial processes.

It aims to create a fully connected and automated production system,
often referred to as a "smart factory". An I4.0 Project must be sustainable
drive change in an organization and enable value creation.

As PM I find myself constantly worked very closely with projects stakeholders,
including business leaders, engineers, data scientists, and software developers,
to define the scope and requirements of a digital solution.

Below I present a series of exercises that describe how to approach the management
of an I4.0 project from the context of the individual implementation of some
of the key digital enablers starting from a fictitious ONE PAGE.


Hyperconnectivity




Hyperconnectivity is a key enabler of I4.0, as it enables the seamless exchange of data and information between different devices and systems.

Big data



Big Data Analytics is a crucial component of Industry 4.0 enabling the collect, store, and analyze vast amounts of data in real-time, allowing to carri out digital solutions that are capable to detect and respond to issues quickly and proactively.
